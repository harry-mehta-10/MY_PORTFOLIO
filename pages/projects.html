2-D PyGame

Developed a classic 2D ping pong game using the PyGame library in Python. Players control paddles to bounce a ball back and forth, aiming to prevent the ball from passing their paddle while attempting to score points by hitting the ball past their opponent's paddle. Also added a few elements to enhance user interactivity. The game features intuitive controls, simple graphics, and engaging gameplay mechanics, providing entertainment and nostalgia for players of all ages.
